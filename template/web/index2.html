Our Story

An Extraordinary
Mountain Sanctuary

Hotel Sarathi is a luxury escape that embodies the finest of nature's grandeur. Just 30 km from Kathmandu, we have been a cherished destination for holidaymakers and event connoisseurs since our opening.

Our expansive, thoughtfully designed architecture and meticulously landscaped grounds combine to create an extraordinary mountain-facing retreat. Dedicated to providing an unparalleled experience — laid-back mountain serenity paired with the finest modern comforts.

Location

Panoramic Mountain Vistas

Dhulikhel's most remarkable feature is undoubtedly its panoramic mountain panorama. On a clear day, visitors behold awe-inspiring views of Annapurna, Langtang, Dorje Lakpa, and Gauri Shankar — with the golden sunrise and sunset particularly captivating.
